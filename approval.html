Approval Voting

Approval voting works by voters selecting all of the candidates they “approve” of.

Pros

  • Prevents “spoiler” effect
  • Increases participation of voters
  • Lowers negative campaigning
  • Lets other candidates get a more accurate degree of support

Cons

  • Voters who know about approval voting will have more strategies in order to help their candidate wins over uneducated voters
